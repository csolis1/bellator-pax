๑ — HISTORY

 THE ART OF
EIGHT LIMBS

Muay Thai is the national sport and cultural martial art of Thailand. Distinguished by its use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins — the eight limbs of the body — it has been practised for centuries and remains one of the most effective stand-up combat systems in the world.

Originating from ancient Siamese military training.

๒ — TECHNIQUES

 STRIKING &
CLINCH WORK

Punches, elbows at five angles, teep (push kicks), roundhouse kicks with the shin, and devastating knee strikes — particularly in the clinch. Footwork and rhythm underpin everything.
