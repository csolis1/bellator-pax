Wing ChunWing Chun

Wing Chun is a concept-based Chinese martial art and form of self-defence utilising both striking and grappling while specialising in close-range combat. The system works on the energy and position of the opponent — using simultaneous defence and attack, centreline theory, economy of movement, and relaxed power to overcome force with structure. Built for the smaller fighter. Perfected over three centuries.
